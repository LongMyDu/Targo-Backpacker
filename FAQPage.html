FAQ

All the most frequent questions, we are here to answer them.

  1. Our hostel address?

    TARGO Backpackers Hostel. 105 Targo street Bundaberg Qld.

  2. What work do you have going on at the moment?

    At the moment wer have picking strawberries. Then after strawberries we have lychees then after lychees we have mangoes.

  3. How much can a person pick per day in average?

    With strawberries currently a person can pick from 200kg to 600kg and on average person can pick 300kg a day.

  4. When does strawberry season end?

    SEPTEMBER / NOVEMBER

  5. How many days a week will we work and how many hours a day?

    At the moment we work 5 to 6 days a week. About 6-9 hours a day from 6am to 1pm. Depending on the weather.

  6. Is the accommodation for boys and girls seperate?

    Yes, it is.

  7. Is the accommodation rate inclusive of the utilities, internet an dother amenities?

    Accommodation include rates and all utilities and amenities include wifi.

  8. How about food?

    You have to get your own food.

  9. Transport from accommodation to work?

    It is $5 a person including drop off and pick up.

  10. How I can get my salary, will it be paid everyday or once a week?

    Salary will be paid weekly in cash envelopes or direct deposit depending on the contractors.
